Welcome to Mountain Mercado, your gateway to the vibrant flavors of New Zealand! Established in 1949, we celebrate the rich culinary heritage of our beautiful country, blending fresh, locally sourced ingredients with innovative cooking techniques.Our menu features a delightful array of dishes inspired by New Zealand’s diverse landscapes, from succulent lamb and seafood to seasonal vegetables. Each dish is crafted with care by our passionate chefs, showcasing the best of our local produce.At Mountain Mercado, we are committed to sustainability and supporting our community. We partner with local farmers and suppliers to ensure our ingredients are fresh and ethically sourced.Whether you’re here for a casual meal or a special occasion, our friendly staff is dedicated to providing a warm and welcoming experience. Join us and discover the unique tastes of New Zealand!
